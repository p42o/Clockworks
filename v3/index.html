07 — Straight answers

The stuff you’d actually ask.

Do I need to buy new software?+

No. I build on what you already run — Jobber, Housecall Pro, QuickBooks, your Google profile, your existing phone number. If you run your business from a notebook and a cell phone, that works too; we start simpler.

I'm not technical. How much do I have to learn?+

Nothing, if you don't want to. No new dashboards, no logins to remember. The systems work inside the tools and the phone you already use, and I set every word they say before anything goes live.

What does it actually cost?+

The audit is free. A pilot — one working system, built and live — is a fixed quote between $2,000 and $5,000 depending on scope. After that, I keep it running, tuned, and improving from $99 to $299 a month. No contracts. One saved job typically covers months of it.

Am I locked in?+

No. You own everything I build — the accounts, the numbers, the logic. Cancel the monthly service anytime and the system keeps working; you'd just be maintaining it yourself.

Will customers know it's AI?+

The texts sound like you because you approve the wording. When a conversation needs a human, it hands off to you fast — the goal is never to fool anyone, it's to answer in seconds instead of never.
