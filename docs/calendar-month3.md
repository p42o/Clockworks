```
⚡ Quick Win: The "iterate" habit

Most people try AI once, get a mediocre result, and stop.

Here's what actually works: treat it like a conversation.

After your first output, say one of these:
→ "Make it shorter"
→ "Make it sound more like me"
→ "Try a different approach"
→ "That's good, but the second paragraph needs to be stronger"
→ "Rewrite this with more urgency"

The second output is almost always better than the first. The third even better.

You're not ordering from a vending machine. You're collaborating.
```
