WELCOME TO THE LAB
your autonomous AI company

Three AI agents run a tiny business for you — they research, build, and prospect on a schedule. But nothing leaves the building without you. Here's the whole thing in 5 tabs:

📥
Inbox — approve or dismiss what they made (digital products, outreach drafts).
🚀
Launch — hand any agent a new gig: “research X, build the smallest sellable version, stage it for me.”
📡
Trends — what's hot right now (live from X + the web). Tap a trend to turn it into a gig.
📨
Mail — email your agents and they reply in a thread. Everything you approve, dispatch, or ask also lands here — so you never have to re-explain anything.
🏭
Rooms — peek at what each agent is doing right now.
🛡️ The Wall: agents can research, build, and draft — but they can never publish, send, or spend. You're always the one who says go.
