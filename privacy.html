Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Clockworks, a doing-business-as name of PGS Ventures LLC, located in Maple Grove, Minnesota, collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@clockworksai.com.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information via Forms

When you submit a contact or booking form on our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Business name and type
  • Description of your business needs or pain points

This information is submitted voluntarily and is stored securely in Firebase/Firestore, a cloud database operated by Google.

Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses localStorage solely to remember your theme preference (light or dark mode). We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies. No personal data is stored in cookies or localStorage.

Automatically Collected Information

Our website does not currently use third-party analytics services. Standard server logs maintained by our hosting provider may record basic connection information such as IP addresses, browser type, and access timestamps. We do not actively collect or analyze this data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or request for a strategy session
  • Schedule and conduct consultation calls
  • Build and deliver automation solutions tailored to your business
  • Communicate with you about your project, deliverables, and support

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services and Platforms

Important Disclaimer: Clockworks implements automation solutions using third-party platforms. None of these solutions are self-hosted by Clockworks. Clockworks is not responsible for any data breaches, service outages, changes in pricing, changes in terms of service, data loss, or other failures caused by third-party providers.

In the course of building and delivering automation solutions, data may be processed through one or more of the following third-party platforms:

  • OpenAI (ChatGPT) — AI language models
  • Google (Gemini) — AI language models
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI language models
  • n8n — workflow automation platform
  • OpenRouter.AI — AI model routing
  • Make.com / Zapier — integration and automation platforms
  • CRM platforms (e.g., HubSpot, GoHighLevel) — customer relationship management
  • Review management platforms — online reputation tools
  • Communication platforms (e.g., Twilio) — SMS, voice, and messaging services
  • Other third-party SaaS tools selected during your strategy session based on your specific business needs

Each of these providers operates under its own privacy policy and terms of service. Data processed through their platforms is subject to their respective policies. We strongly recommend that clients review each provider's privacy policy and terms of service independently.

Clockworks does not control how third-party providers handle, store, or process data once it enters their systems. We select reputable providers and configure integrations following best practices, but we cannot guarantee their performance, security, or compliance.

4. Security Measures

Clockworks takes security seriously and implements all available security measures on the third-party platforms we configure for clients, including:

  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) on all accounts where available
  • Login notifications to alert account owners of new sign-ins
  • IP allowlisting where supported by the platform
  • Role-based access controls to limit permissions to only what is necessary
  • API key rotation following best practices for credential management
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data processed through third-party platforms.

5. Client Access and Credentials

During the implementation of automation solutions, Clockworks may be given login credentials or administrative access to client accounts in order to configure integrations and automations. All such access is used solely for the purpose of building and maintaining the agreed-upon automations.

Offboarding

Upon completion of services or at the client's request, Clockworks will:

  • Remove all Clockworks access from client accounts
  • Delete any stored credentials related to the client's accounts
  • Provide documentation of all integrations and automations configured
  • Transfer full ownership and control of all systems to the client

Clients may request offboarding at any time by contacting hello@clockworksai.com.

6. Data Retention

  • Form submissions (name, email, business details) are retained for the purpose of business communication and follow-up.
  • Client project data is retained only for the duration of active service engagement.
  • Clients may request deletion of their personal data at any time by emailing hello@clockworksai.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@clockworksai.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

Clockworks reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@clockworksai.com
  • Business: Clockworks (PGS Ventures LLC)
  • Location: Maple Grove, Minnesota
