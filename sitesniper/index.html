How it works

Three steps. One tool.

01

Point it at any target.

Paste any small business URL. Site Sniper scrapes their photos, colors, fonts, copy, hours, and contact info. Then audits the site across SEO, AIO, performance, accessibility, and trust — five categories, one 0-100 score.

02

03

Send the pitch.

The cold-outreach generator writes a Facebook DM, LinkedIn message, and SMS tuned to that exact business. Send the link with the PIN. When they say yes, an onboarding form and a host-specific deploy guide are auto-generated.
